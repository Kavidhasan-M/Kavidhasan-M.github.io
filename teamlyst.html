Problem

Students have trouble finding people who are interested to work as team with them on their idea.

When students want to work on any projects with other students, there is no way to find people to work with, or available projects to join when they don't have an idea themselves.

Research

Quantitative Analysis

I made a survey using Google Forms to get some insights and got 58 responses from college students.

Qualitative Analysis

I conducted user interviews with 11 students.

Key takeaways

Userflow

Features

Depending on the tasks that users will carry out on this platform, the following tasks are implemented as features,

  • Explore the projects available to join in the team.
  • Search people based on skills.
  • Post about the project they are working on or about to work with roles available to join the team.
  • Status about their request to the team builder.

Prototype

The user interview allowed me to know that every student had a specific purpose on involving in a project and this let me come up with the types of project. Their expectations on building/joining a team were like,

  • Projects that can help them show their skills and ability during placements.
  • Want to participate in hackathon and other events.
  • For startups and the activities that they are passionate about beyond the academics.
